## Technical

Shipping AI Agent Safety Toolkit: a docs-first umbrella for three small tools:

- BountyLens: bounty issue risk triage
- Testability Doctor: repo testability preflight
- AgentGate: pre-PR diff safety gate

Designed for Codex, Claude Code, Cursor, and human review.

## Safety-Focused

AI coding agents need guardrails before the PR, not only after.

AI Agent Safety Toolkit separates the workflow:

- check the issue
- check repo testability
- check the diff
- open draft PR
- keep human review

No auto-claim, no payout handling, no review replacement.
